About me

I am a research scientist in the FAIR team at Meta (Paris). Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the same team at Meta and in the SCOOL team at INRIA Lille. I received my Ph.D. in computer science at Politecnico di Milano advised by Marcello Restelli. My main research interest is reinforcement learning.
